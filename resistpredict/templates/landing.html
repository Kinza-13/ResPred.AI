ResistPredict.AI

AI prediction of Resistance and Virulence in bacterial pathogens

ResistPredict.AI utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to predict antibiotic resistance genes (ARGs) and virulence factors (VFs) in bacterial pathogens. It incorporates continuously updated, diverse datasets for broader applicability across bacterial species to help researchers and healthcare workers fight with antibiotic resistance.
